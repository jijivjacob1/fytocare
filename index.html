FYTOCARE CHEMICALS The Pesticide Solutions Company

Fytocare Chemicals Pvt. Ltd., was founded with the vision of enabling an insect/rodent free space in the most efficient and hygienic manner. Over the years, the company has built a loyal clientele of established market leaders who market our products under their brand name. Our products enjoy a significant share in the total volume of chalk and rodenticide formulations manufactured in India

Set up in 1994 by Dr. Raghunath Thompson, an industry veteran, Fytocare Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. has been engaged in the formulation and sale of household insecticides, including insecticide chalks and aerosols, besides manufacturing rodenticide including Bromadiolone technical and Bromadiolone formulations. All these products are covered by the Insecticide Act 1968 and registered with the Central Insecticide Board, Faridabad. Fytocare Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. is a member of Chemexcil and The Indian Chamber of Commerce and Industry.

What we do

At Fytocare Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. we primarily produce insecticide chalks and rodenticide formulations for our client companies, who market them under their trade name. Our products are supplied to FMCG giants, besides pest control operators.

Our chalk formulations account for 50% and our rodenticide formulations account for about 25% of the total volume manufactured in India. Our clients include Godrej Consumer Products Pvt. Ltd. and Hicare Services Pvt. Ltd., among others.

The products we manufacture conform to stringent quality guidelines and are manufactured under expert supervision.

Fytocare Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. has the registration for Cypermethrin 1% chalk, Deltamethrin 0.5% chalk and Lambda Cyhalothrin 0.5% chalk, Bromadiolone technical, Bromadiolone 0.25% liquid and powder concentrate, Bromadiolone 0.005% wax block and Bromadiolone 0.005% grain bait.

Our Facility

Our manufacturing process combines technical efficiency with environmental responsibility. Located over 14 acres in Coimbatore's south taluk, between Malumichampatti and Bodipalayam villages, Fytocare's manufacturing area spans 30, 000 sq. feet. Bulk of the land is covered by a permanent stretch of trees, providing ample green cover.

We have state-of-the-art blending and moulding machinery for chalk production, reactors, boilers, chillers, driers and centrifuges for the technical manufacture of Bromadiolone, which ensure optimized utilization of resources. And our automatic packing machines, homogenisers, extruders, cutting and stamping machinery facilitate timely and quality-driven production of rodenticide formulation.
